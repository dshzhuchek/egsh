ABOUT ME
I work with sound for film, animation, games and multimedia.
I work on projects in film, animation, music production, and media as a sound designer, sound engineer, and musician. I also work on music videos and develop sound design for advertising productions.

For my work in film, animation, and media, I use Reaper, Pro Tools, Logic Pro, FMOD and Wwise for game audio.

I enjoy trying unconventional approaches, developing new ideas, and implementing interesting concepts in sound creation. I create many textures manually, recording sounds in the studio, or using plug-ins for sound synthesis.

I approach projects with special attention and creativity, and am always open to discussion.

In my work, it is important for me to convey emotions and a sense of presence through sound, to saturate the visual with a textured atmosphere and unique sound.
